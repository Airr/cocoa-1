The alderg-kmer and its companion command-line alder-kmer are made to be helpful in counting kmer sequences in FASTA or FASTQ sequence files. This program would be ideal for computers with multiple-CPUs, lower-memory, and larger disk space.

# References
1. Rizk, Lavenier and Chikhi. 2013 Bioinformatics 29:652-653. DSK: k-mer counting with very low memory usage.
2. Marcais and Kingsford. 2011 Bioinformatics 27:764-770. A fast, lock-free approach for efficient parallel counting of occurrences of k-mers.
